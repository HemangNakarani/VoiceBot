Welcome to VoiceBot

Voice bot is a voice assistant for the Sprinklr platform, where you can
give commands to it by your voice. It will understand your commands and
execute them.

Modules

Editorial Calendar
Assest Management
Ads Manager
Care Console

Help

  • Click on the extension Button to show/hide Voicebot interface in Sprinklr Platform

  • Click on Microphone to give commands to open Modules from Home Page,
    No need to stop Microphone, it will automatically do.

  • Once you entered in Module you can give specific commands to apply filter in that module

  • Scroll down in the bot interface to see the Settings Page

  • Use Drag Button in the left corner to rearrage the bot interface anywhere on website

  • Use minimize button to hide bot interface

  • type "vb" on Ominbox and hit space bar.
    Now type "open" and hit enter to open sprinklr website or use "help" to open this page again
